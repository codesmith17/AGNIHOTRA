Healing through Fire

Agnihotra is a healing fire from the ancient science of Ayurveda. It is a process of purifying the atmosphere through a specially prepared fire performed daily at sunrise and sunset.

Get Timings

What is Agnihotra?

Nature's Rhythm

Performed exactly at the transition from night to day and day to night, capturing the peak energy of our planet.

Atmospheric Purification

The copper pyramid and specific materials create a resonance that heals the environment, plants, and humans.

Total Healing

The ash produced is a powerful fertilizer and medicine, containing all 92 natural elements in a highly bio-available form.

Five Essential Materials

The specific combination of these five elements creates the powerful healing atmosphere of Agnihotra.

Copper Pyramid

A vessel of specific size and shape. Copper is a conductor for subtle energies and its pyramid shape generates resonance.

Dried Cow Dung

Used as fuel. It contains a wealth of medicinal properties and burns with a pure, therapeutic smoke.

Pure Cow Ghee

Clarified butter from cow's milk. It acts as a powerful catalyst, releasing medicinal properties into the air.

Unpolished Rice

Two pinches of whole, unbroken grains. Rice symbolizes the seed of life and holds the vibrational charge of the mantras.

Precise Timing

The transition moments of sunrise and sunset. This synchronization allows the fire to capture peak solar energy.

        Sūrya Mantra

        सूर्याय स्वाहा । सूर्याय इदं न मम ।

        प्रजापतये स्वाहा । प्रजापतये इदं न मम ॥
